Hi!

Thanks for stopping by. My name is Kellen and I built this website to give you a glimpse into my professional life. I’m a freelance Product Designer and aspiring Software Engineer working remotely out of Seattle. For me, the collaborative process of understanding a problem and the iterative process of solving it are genuinely fun. I'm someone who instinctively digs deeper to find the root cause of an issue and I love the variability that working for users affords. I get excited about designing anything from a logo to the schema of a database, to the component hierarchy of a React app.

Previously, I was working as a Geographer at Expedia. Before geography-ing, I did LiDAR Analysis for GSi. Prior to that I was a student at the University of Washington where my interest and education in code and design began. Before attending UW I spent 12 years as a musician. It was during this time that my creativity, empathy, and collaborative spirit were shaped.

I'm always trying to grow by learning something new, and finding new ways to challenge myself. I always seem to lose track of time when I'm writing code, working on a design, enjoying good conversation, or bingeing the latest series with my wife and dog.

Currently, I'm a full-stack software engineering student at Rithm School, but am open to fun side projects. Please don't hesitate to reach out— I'm happy to chat about work or play!
