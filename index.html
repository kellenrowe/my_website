Hi!

Thanks for stopping by. I’m a freelance Product Designer working remotely out of Seattle. For me, the process of understanding is just as fun as the process of solving. Curious by nature, I'm someone who instinctively digs deeper to find the root cause of an issue and I love the variability that working for users affords. I get excited about conducting research and brainstorming solutions. Collaboration and iteration are my jams.

Previously, I was working as a Geographer at Expedia. Before geography-ing, I did LiDAR Analysis. Prior to that I was a student at the University of Washington where my interest and education in code and design began. Before attending UW I spent 12 years as a musician. It was during this time that my creativity, empathy, and collaborative spirit were shaped.

I'm always trying to grow by learning something new, and finding new ways to challenge myself. I always seem to lose track of time when I'm working on design, writing, enjoying good conversation, or exploring places I've never been.

Currently, I'm looking for new opportunities, and I am open to fun side projects. Please don't hesitate to reach out— I'm happy to chat about work or play!
