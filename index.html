Hi!

Thanks for stopping by. My name is Kellen and I built this website to give you a glimpse into my professional life. I’m a lot of things and a software engineer is one of them. Being a software engineer is awesome because there is effectively no limit to how much you can learn. It's a beautifully frustrating and rewarding process.

Previously, I was working as UX Designer at Cloverbook which followed my time as a Geographer at Expedia. Before geography-ing, I did LiDAR Analysis for GSi. Prior to that I was a student at the University of Washington where my interest and education in code and design began. Before attending UW I spent 10 years as a musician. It was during this time that my creativity, critical thinking, and collaborative spirit were shaped.

I'm always trying to grow by learning something new, and finding new ways to challenge myself. I seem to lose track of time when I'm writing code, working on a design, enjoying good conversation, or bingeing the latest series with my wife and dog.

Recently, I completed a Software Engineer Internship with Livestack, where I helped creators connect with their audience, despite Covid-19 preventing social gathering. I'm currently developing an internal communications app for a Seattle based company and am actively seeking my next opportunity. Please don't hesitate to reach out— I'm happy to chat about work or play!
