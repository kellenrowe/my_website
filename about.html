Kellen Rowe is a University of Washington graduate with a degree in Geographic Information Systems.

Through a blend of formal and independent-study, Kellen learned to use HTML, CSS, and Java, implementing each to build this and other websites.

Having spent the past 12 years dedicated to another career path, he received an extremely well-rounded education - one which goes beyond traditional didactics, and encompasses learning through experience, self-reflection, and exposure to new personal, professional, and cultural horizons. Kellen Rowe brings a unique perspective to the industry and aims to maximize the relationship between companies, creators, and users.

Pursuing a career in technology development, specifically UX Design, he is actively seeking his next opportunity to be creatively solving problems with a team of unique individuals.
