About this Project

Under the psuedonym Art of Atlas, West Coast artist, Wesley Ayers creates from a perspective of loss. Two years ago, after suffering from Lyme disease and losing his girlfriend in a car accident and his grandmother soon afterwards, Ayers chose to dedicate his life to his dream of becoming an artist and a writer.

Westley reached out to me with a graphic design inquiry in September of 2019. I was touched by his story, and offered to take on the project pro bono.
